Summer Research Project (2023) under the Supervision of Dr. Ciaran O'Hare at the University of Sydney. 

A lightweight Python project for modeling dark matter direct detection from first principles.

This repository implements:
- Standard Halo Model velocity integrals
- Helm nuclear form factors
- Spin-independent recoil spectra
- Event-rate calculations for xenon detectors
- Exposure and threshold studies
- Sensitivity projections and comparisons to LZ limits and the neutrino floor

Designed as a transparent phenomenology sandbox that can be extended toward more realistic detector simulations.
